## Tu hoc kin den hoc mo

Truoc day minh hoc kha nhieu, nhung ket qua roi rac va kho giu nhiet. Khi bat dau build in public, moi thu thay doi vi:

- Minh buoc phai ship de co cai ma chia se.
- Minh nhan feedback that som.
- Minh co ap luc tich cuc de lam den noi den chon.

## Build in public khong phai show-off

Voi minh, build in public la cach tao accountability.

### Accountability ve output

Neu da public roadmap, minh it bo dang hon.

### Accountability ve chat luong

Khi nguoi khac co the doc code/doc bai, minh can viet ro rang hon.

## 3 tac dong ro nhat

### 1. Hoc nhanh hon

Vi moi bai hoc deu can bien thanh output, minh cat bo duoc viec "doc cho vui".

### 2. Nho lau hon

Khi phai giai thich lai, cac concept kho nhu crawl pipeline hay API auth tro nen ro hon rat nhieu.

### 3. Tang authority tu nhung viec nho

Authority khong den tu mot bai viral, ma den tu consistency va do huu ich.

## Cach bat dau nhe nhang

- Moi tuan ship 1 bai ngan.
- Moi thang ship 1 mini tool.
- Moi bai deu co "what worked / what failed".
